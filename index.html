General Institute Requirements

Science Core

  • Mathematics: 18.01 Calculus I and 18.02 Calculus II (or approved variants).
  • Physics: 8.01 Physics I and 8.02 Physics II (or variants).
  • Chemistry: 5.111/5.112 Principles of Chemical Science or 3.091 Introduction to Solid-State Chemistry.
  • Biology: 7.012, 7.015, 7.014, or 7.016.

Laboratory

Complete at least one designated laboratory subject.

REST

Take two Restricted Electives in Science and Technology. Examples include 6.1200 Mathematics for Computer Science, 18.03 Differential Equations, and 6.3000 Signal Processing.

Communication Requirement

Four Communication-Intensive subjects: two CI-H or CI-HW and two CI-M in the major. Having passed the FEE, any CI-H or CI-HW may be taken in the first year.

HASS Requirement

  • Eight Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences subjects.
  • Distribution: one each from HASS-H, HASS-A, and HASS-S.
  • Concentration: three or four subjects in one field plus one or two additional HASS electives.
  • At least two HASS subjects must also be CI-H or CI-HW, with the first completed in the first year.

Physical Education & Wellness

Swim test and four quarters of physical education (crew satisfies this requirement).
